Fiji

About Fiji

We have all seen them, the alluring pictures of white beaches with a turquoise sea meeting a cloudless blue sky. Perhaps a few exotic palms offering shade to a couple of large, sedentary turtles. It looks like the perfect place for the holiday of our dreams. In fact, Fiji has far more to offer than picture perfect postcards. Here are some things you didn't know about Fiji!

Fiji facts

Fiji is a South Pacific archipelago, about 2,000 km from New Zealand, with roughly 300 volcanic islands and over 500 smaller islets.
The main islands, Viti Levu and Vanua Levu, house most of Fiji’s nearly one million people, with the capital, Suva, located on Viti Levu. Fiji’s climate is tropical marine, with a wet season (November to April) and a dry season (May to October), occasionally affected by cyclones.

Isolated from other lands, Fiji hosts unique plant and animal species, with rainforests covering about half its landmass and rich marine ecosystems. Settled from Taiwan thousands of years ago, Fiji became a British colony in 1874 and achieved independence in 1970.

Reasons to visit Fiji

Beaches

  • White sand
  • Sunbathing
  • Variety


Fiji is home to an array of beautiful beaches, ensuring there's a perfect spot for everyone. Whether you're chasing thrilling waves to surf, luxuriating in the sun on soft sands, or seeking a lively place to play, you'll find your paradise here. With crystal-clear waters and breathtaking views, Fiji's beaches invite you to unwind and bask in the beauty of island life!

Snorkling & Scuba diving

  • Marine life
  • Spear fishing
  • Snorkel with sharks


Explore the wonders beneath the waves with unforgettable adventures in the ocean! Snorkeling and scuba diving reveal vibrant marine life, from colorful corals to exotic fish darting through crystal-clear waters. For those seeking a thrill, spear fishing offers an exciting challenge, while snorkeling with sharks provides a breathtaking encounter with these majestic creatures.

Island jumping

  • Culture
  • Private paradise
  • Sailing


Set sail and explore the charm of island hopping, where each shore unveils unique cultures, stunning views, and warm welcomes. Find your own slice of paradise, drift along turquoise waters, and immerse yourself in the vibrant spirit of each island. Adventure and serenity await with every wave!

Hiking

  • Experiense the jungle
  • Climb mountains
  • Wildlife tours


Embark on a journey through lush jungles, where every trail reveals the thrill of hiking amidst dense, vibrant greenery. Climb mountains for breathtaking views and join wildlife tours to encounter the region’s unique animals up close. Adventure awaits in the heart of the wild!
