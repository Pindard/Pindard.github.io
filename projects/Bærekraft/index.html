What happens to nuclear waste?

Nuclear power has been considered a sustainable energy source due to its low greenhouse gas emissions and its ability to produce large amounts of energy. However, an important question arises.

How can nuclear power truly be sustainable when it leaves behind large amounts of dangerous waste that must be managed for thousands of years?
